About Seattle University

Seattle University, founded in 1891, is a Jesuit Catholic university located on 50 acres in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood.

In U.S. News & World Report's Best Colleges 2013, Seattle University continues to rank in the top 10 in the West. See full rankings.
